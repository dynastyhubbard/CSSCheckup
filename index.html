Online Stores versus Storefronts

Online Stores

Online store can be managed from any where, any place. All you need is a computer and right team or protocols set up to to run your business smoothly and you can see yourself working as little as 5 hours a week.

Online stores can be made from scratch or they can be made on certain sites. Website building sites can be made from Wix, Wordpress, Shopify, Weebly, Bigcartel, etc. These stores to sell on a pre-made platform includ amazon, esty, ebay, etc.

Storefronts used to be the only way to sell, but with the new age of the internet being so easily accesible around the world you now have more options.

There are times an actual store would trump an oline store especially dealing with items that have short exiration dates or items that may need to be prepared/served to the customer.

In todays world though, I could give you 50+ alternatives as to why you can invest what you would into a store front for just a regular business space where you can prepare your products if needed and get them same day shipped as well.
